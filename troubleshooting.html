--- layout: markdown-page trail: [main] title: Troubleshooting categories: [general] --- First, don't panic :) Second, dfhack keeps a few log files in DF's folder (`stderr.log` and `stdout.log`). Looking at these might help you solve the problem. If it doesn't, you can ask for help in the forum thread or on IRC. If you found a bug, you can report it in the Bay12 DFHack thread, the issues tracker on Github, or visit the #dfhack IRC channel on freenode.
